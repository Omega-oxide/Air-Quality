Intro

Welcome to our website on our Raspberry Pi project using the BME230 Sensor

We are the Air Quality Team (Very creative I know)
Our team members are as follows: Jennifer Pumphrey, Taylin Roberts, and Benjamin Green

For our project, we made a temperature and humidity sensor using a Raspberry Pi that was integrated with an Adafruit BME280 sensor. This sensor monitors the temperature and humidity of the environment around itself and reports this data. Temperature and humidity impact the air quality and a need for affordable and reliable air quality systems grows. Our project addresses this need with the development of an air quality sensor that is low cost using Raspberry Pi and has an accurate collection of real-time data for checking air quality.
