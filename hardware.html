It is a decent board despite its issues. One of which was the proprietary micro cords which were a-bit trouble to get a hold of. Online is probably the only good place to get them so look there first.
Lastly third party cheap cords, such as the usb coupling, failed to yield any hardware interactivity.

The Air Quality does advocate for getting and working with a board like this, just know the hoops you may have to jump throw for it to work with your own hardware on hand
